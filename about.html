About

what inspired this project

This website is a collaborative effort between Mohashin Azad and Roger Antigua for our Interactive Narrative, Spring 2015 final project. The goal of this website is to provide a platform in which a large group of people, in this case our class, will create a unique story together in an organized matter. The twist, however, is that by the site's design, only one person may go at a time, and only the part that was submitted to the website before their turn is visible to them as reference. With that in mind, think of this website as a combination of "Mad Libs" and "Telephone.

This format will encourage each user to get creative, and is an experiment to see just how everyone can work to progress the story despite their virtual limits, as well as an experiment to see how design and surroundings affect each person's individual progress. Once every participant has gone at least one time, the story is 'completed' regardless of its current state, and the entire sequence is saved for the purpose of examination.
